    ABOUT

    TekknoSalads are dancing on the Ethereum blockchain and represent how our being changes AT and AFTER a successful rave.

    We are all lettuce leaves

    Walking into the club (aka salad spinner) feeling crisp and fresh, happy to go nuts for a few hours with our salad friends, and not leaving until completely washed-out. Satisfied with the physical and mental workout, a refreshingly cool bath will revive us back to life.

    Each salad is a unique artwork created by a passionate artist and rave-girl.

    5.859 salads are available in public sale and will be generated randomly and revealed after your purchase. Thrown together from various hand-drawn traits with different raritys to represent the diversity and uniqeness of existence.

    HOW TO BUY A TEKKNOSALAD NFT?

    Connect your Metamask wallet on our website and click on the "mint" button. Choose the amount of TekknoSalads you wish to mint and they will then appear in your wallet and on Opensea.
